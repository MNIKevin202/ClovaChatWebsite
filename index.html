FAQ

Questions

Can I use ClovaChat for free?

Yes. The free version lets you connect one server/account, autojoin up to two channels, create two popups, three scripts, and one timer.

Is Premium a subscription?

No. Lifetime Premium is a one-time $29.99 purchase, plus tax where applicable.

What does Premium unlock?

Unlimited servers, accounts, autojoin channels, popups, scripts, timers, chat logging, emotes, and the full dashboard.

Is ClovaChat for streamers only?

No. It is useful for streamers, moderators, and Twitch chat regulars who want a cleaner desktop workflow.
